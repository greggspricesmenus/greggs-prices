Greggs Eat-In and Delivery Prices

Two situations where Greggs prices run higher than the standard takeaway rate are worth knowing before you order.

Eat-in: If you sit down inside a Greggs with seating, a 20 percent VAT surcharge applies to all food and hot drinks under UK tax rules. A £1.35 Sausage Roll taken away stays at £1.35. The same roll eaten in would cost around £1.62. Most Greggs stores do not have dedicated seating, so this rarely comes up, but it matters at larger format locations.

Delivery: Ordering through Just Eat or Uber Eats adds a platform markup of roughly 15 to 20 percent on top of the in-store price. Ordering through the Greggs app directly for Click and Collect avoids that markup and keeps prices at the standard in-store rate.
